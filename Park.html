Our Mission

Quickly after the founding of our city, the council began to take notice of the influx and growth of families in our community. As the city continued to tackle the issues of a new community, there was was one that was most observable. Our residents, from the children to the elderly, were without the structure of an organized parks and recreational system. The council then found the Parks and Recreations department with hopes to improve this issue. Our goal was to advance the development of a safe community, with many amentities for our many residents. Successfully, we have created over 20 parks and facilities, adding new programs and events weekly all at the service of our city and wonderful residents.
